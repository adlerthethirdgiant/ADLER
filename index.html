We Are Adler

Code of Conduct

Adler is a community of dedicated racers who choose to be here. Rather than imposing complex hierarchies, rigid requirements, or bureaucratic systems that demand constant enforcement and interpretation, we have embraced a simple philosophical principle that serves the growth of all. Therefore, our entire approach may be condensed into one essential commitment:

Cultivate social interest by contributing to the welfare of others and the community, recognizing that your own progress is inseparable from the common good.

Common Sense Introduction

  • If you choose to join the Adler team, you are welcomed and encouraged to embrace our principles of purposeful growth and social contribution.
  • When seeking guidance, study the fundamentals, practice deliberately, and provide context about your progress and challenges.
  • When offering help, be patient and constructive‚Äîremember that every master was once a beginner.
  • Strive courageously for your own mastery while contributing meaningfully to the progress of others.
