Team Captain's Transparency

Hey Adlerians,

I want to be real with you all. As our team keeps growing, I think you deserve to know the full story behind where we are today.

My Journey

I started Nitro Type on October 3rd, 2022, knowing absolutely nothing about proper typing. I learned QWERTY from scratch, drilled the homerow until my fingers hurt, and raced constantly to build speed. By April 1st, 2023, I hit my personal best of 122 WPM—every single word earned through practice, not shortcuts. I need you to know: I've never used an auto-typer or any bot. This team was built the hard way—blood, sweat, and tears. Every stat you see from me is legitimate, and that's a promise I make to each of you.

Where I Am Now?

In Sep 2025, I made the tough decision to switch to Dvorak layout. My speeds have dropped significantly while I relearn everything, but I'm committed to the long-term benefits. It's humbling, honestly, but it reminds me that improvement is always possible.

Why I'm Sharing This?

Honesty: You deserve a captain who leads by example and tells the truth

Community Building:When we're open about our struggles and wins, we grow stronger together

Accountability: This transparency holds me accountable to all of you and encourages me to continually strive for improvement.

My stats are posted here for anyone to verify. I encourage you all to share yours too—not to compete, but to celebrate how far we've all come.

This team matters to me. You all matter to me. Let's keep building something we can be proud of.

Let's keep racing forward—the right way.
