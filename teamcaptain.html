ADLER — Team Captain's Journey

Hello everyone,

As your team captain, I believe in the importance of transparency and sharing our journeys together. Since I started playing Nitro Type in October 2022, I have learned a lot about typing, racing, and improving my skills. I want to share my progress with you to inspire and encourage everyone on our team.

My Journey

Initial Skills: When I first began playing, I focused on mastering the basics of typing. I quickly learned the QWERTY and Homerow keys, which laid the foundation for my typing speed.

Current Progress: I’m proud to say that my highest recorded typing speed is 122 WPM. This achievement reflects my dedication and practice over the past few years.

Recent Changes

New Challenges: As of 2025, I decided to switch to the Dvorak keyboard layout. This has required a lot of adjustment and practice, but I believe it will ultimately enhance my typing efficiency and speed.

Stat Sharing: To back up my claims, I’m sharing my stats here for everyone to see. I encourage each of you to do the same, as it creates a culture of openness and motivation within our team.

Why This Matters

Inspiration: By sharing my journey, I hope to inspire each of you to embrace your own paths to improvement.

Community Building: Openly discussing our stats fosters a sense of camaraderie. We can learn from one another and celebrate our successes together.

Accountability: This transparency holds me accountable to all of you and encourages me to continually strive for improvement.

Together, as a team, we can support each other in our goals and become even better racers and typists. I’m looking forward to hearing about your journeys and stats too!

Let’s keep racing forward!
